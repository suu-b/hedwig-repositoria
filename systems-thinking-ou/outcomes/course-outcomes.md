# Course Outcomes

- <u>Challenge</u> systematic thinking and approach to complex situations.
- <u>Explain</u> how and why different systems of interest can be defined and described withing complex situations.
- <u>Recognize</u> each person brings their own perspective.
- <u>Work</u> with those multiple perspectives.
- <u>Relate</u> key ideas, techniques, and approaches in systems thinking to professional practice when working with complex situations.
- <u>Plan</u> how to take the study of systems thinking in practice further for professional developement.
