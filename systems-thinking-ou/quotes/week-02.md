# Week 2 — Quotes

**On feedback delays:**

> "The most dangerous feature of a feedback loop is not its direction but its delay. A delay turns a balancing loop into an oscillating one." — Instructor, Week 2

**On mental models:**

> "We do not act on reality. We act on our mental models of reality. Systems thinking is, at its core, an upgrade to those models." — Course Material, Unit 2.1
